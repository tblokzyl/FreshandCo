About Us

Fresh & Co was founded in 2001 by Al Smithee, Ally Smithy, Alan Smith, and Alvin Smitt, four individuals with a knack for cooking, and a hack for getting it done efficiently. After they grew tired of spending every day at work, and coming home exhausted, a brilliant idea was formed. A dinner delivery service to assist other individuals and families with the same dilemna. Because, let’s face it - there’s just not enough hours in the day to cook five star meals, every night.

Our meals are prepared every night by five star chefs in our five star home restaurant. We use only the finest and freshest ingredients, with vegetables provided from local farms, bread baked daily on the premises, and pasta hand rolled by our master Italian chefs. We do it all so you don’t need to.

We have over 500 weekly subscribers, and are growing daily. We have multiple delivery drivers who work with us, so we can ensure that you get your dinner when you want it, hot and ready to eat. Because after all, that’s what we’re about.
